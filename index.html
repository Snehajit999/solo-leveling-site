Solo Leveling (Korean: 나 혼자만 레벨업) is a South Korean fantasy web novel written by Chugong. Serialized on KakaoPage from July 25, 2016, and published by D&C Media under the Papyrus label. Licensed in English by Yen Press.

A webtoon adaptation illustrated by Jang Sung-rak (Dubu) debuted March 4, 2018 on KakaoPage — Season 1 concluded March 19, 2020. An anime series by A-1 Pictures aired January–March 2024, with Season 2 Arise from the Shadow airing January–March 2025.

// 02 — Plot
Rise from
the shadow
In a world where hunters — humans with supernatural abilities — battle deadly monsters to protect mankind, a notoriously weak hunter named Sung Jin-woo finds himself in a seemingly endless struggle for survival.
After narrowly surviving an overwhelming double dungeon, a mysterious program called The System selects him as its sole player — granting him the unique power to level up, something no other hunter can do. He discovers he has been chosen to inherit the position of Shadow Monarch — an immortal necromancer, the King of the Dead — created by a god during the war between the Rulers and the Monarchs. He is the only Monarch who fights to save humanity.

// 03 — Characters
The players
of the system
Sung Jin-woo
Protagonist · Shadow Monarch · E-Rank → Beyond National Level
Voiced by Taito Ban (JP) · Aleks Le (EN) · Portrayed by Byeon Woo-seok

Originally the weakest E-Rank hunter in South Korea, chosen as sole Player by The System. He rises to surpass all National Level Hunters and inherits the title of Shadow Monarch from Ashborn. Marries S-Rank hunter Cha Hae-in and has a son, Suho. In the sequel Ragnarok, he leaves Earth to fight the Itarim alongside the Rulers.
Cha Hae-in
S-Rank Hunter · Wife of Sung Jin-woo
One of South Korea's most powerful S-Rank hunters. Eventually marries Sung Jin-woo and becomes the mother of Sung Suho.
Ashborn
Original Shadow Monarch · King of the Dead
The original holder of the Shadow Monarch title, created by a god during the war between Rulers and Monarchs. Chose Jin-woo as his successor to carry on the mantle.
Sung Suho
Son of Sung Jin-woo · Protagonist of Ragnarok
Son of Jin-woo and Cha Hae-in, who inherits some of his father's powers. In Solo Leveling: Ragnarok, Beru unseals his memories and powers to defend Earth from the Itarim invasion.
